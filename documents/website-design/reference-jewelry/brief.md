## Step 2 : Research (direction note)

References studied (Awwwards + galleries): Sasai (handcrafted jewelry e-commerce), Floema (WebGL jewelry brand experience, unusual navigation), Aroz (fashion-clean jewelry e-commerce), plus Awwwards editorial-layout and dark-mode collections.

```
WHAT WORKS:    editorial-magazine layout with oversized type as the structure; product shot
               like an art object (multiple angles, macro that shows METAL TEXTURE); brand-heritage
               / craft storytelling; experimental but legible navigation.
WHAT TO AVOID: soft-pastel "accessible luxury lifestyle" (now a cliche); the generic centered
               product grid; and our own ledger neighbors (light-cosmetic, candlelit-dark).
STEAL LIST:    1) editorial oversized Cormorant as the page skeleton (asymmetric, broken grid)
               2) macro texture imagery (molten gold, hammered metal, raw stone) as atmosphere
               3) a draggable / horizontal editorial collection gallery (Floema-ish, CSS/JS)
               4) maker's-hands (no face) forge storytelling
               5) one dramatic near-black "forge" gallery band amid warm clay/cream sections
```

## Step 3 : Art direction (the POV)

```
POV:           Editorial-materico maximalism : "l'oro nasce dal fuoco". A hand-printed forge
               catalogue: warm clay + cream + ink, molten gold, a garnet jewel accent, real
               texture everywhere, type oversized like a magazine cover.
SIGNATURE:     the forge hero : a single hero piece in a warm spotlight on hammered/molten
               texture, the name BRACE set in giant Cormorant across an asymmetric editorial
               split, ember glow + grain. Plus a draggable editorial collection gallery.
TYPE:          Cormorant Garamond (display, up to huge, light + bold, italics) + Figtree (body).
COLOR:         ground warm cream/bone + bold clay/terracotta brand field; ink text; molten gold
               the single CTA accent; garnet for tiny accents. One dramatic ember-dark band.
ATMOSPHERE:    paper/plaster grain, warm vignettes, soft ember glow on the dark band, gold leaf
               texture; depth via layered warm light, never a flat fill.
COMPOSITION:   editorial broken grid : oversized type, off-axis pieces, overlap, generous space;
               sections alternate cream / clay / ember-dark; the eye zig-zags.
MOTION:        one orchestrated load (cascade on the forge hero) + an ember glow that breathes
               (dial 4, reduced-motion-safe) + draggable gallery + reveal on scroll. No counters.
SECTION-INTRO: kicker with a thin gold rule + a roman numeral (I, II, III). Not a dotted eyebrow.
```
