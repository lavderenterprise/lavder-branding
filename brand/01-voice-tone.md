# Voice & Tone

## Voice

Voice is what we sound like, always. It does not change across channels.

**Lavder sounds like a senior operator.** Someone who has built the thing you're trying to build, knows where it breaks, and respects your time enough to tell you plainly.

Three traits, always present:

1. **Direct.** We use subject–verb–object. We don't pad with "we believe", "we feel", "in our opinion". If we say it, we believe it.
2. **Confident without swagger.** We do not hedge ("maybe", "kind of", "hopefully"). We do not peacock ("revolutionary", "game-changing", "industry-leading"). We state facts.
3. **Warm without performing.** We are friendly because we respect the reader, not because a brand guideline told us to use exclamation points.

## Tone

Tone is voice adjusted for context. It moves along two axes:

| Context          | Formal ↔ Plain | Serious ↔ Playful |
|------------------|----------------|-------------------|
| Legal & contract | Formal         | Serious           |
| Sales pitch      | Formal-ish     | Serious           |
| Marketing page   | Plain          | Mostly serious    |
| Product UI copy  | Plain          | Neutral           |
| Empty/error state| Plain          | Slight warmth     |
| Internal Slack   | Plain          | Playful OK        |

## Do

- Write sentences a CEO would read without skimming.
- Lead with the verb. *Ship a site in 30 days* > *We'll help you with shipping a site, possibly in 30 days.*
- Cut every word that does not earn its place.
- Use concrete nouns and numbers. *47 sites shipped in 2025* > *many successful projects*.
- Prefer active voice. *We broke the record* > *The record was broken by us*.

## Don't

- **No corporate filler.** Never write "synergy", "solutions", "leverage", "best-in-class", "next-generation", "cutting-edge", "holistic", "ecosystem".
- **No startup-boomer clichés.** Never "game-changer", "disruptor", "10x", "paradigm shift", "unleash".
- **No emoji in formal surfaces.** OK in internal tools and in-product moments. Never in brand pages, legal copy, sales material.
- **No em-dash addiction.** Use full stops. One idea per sentence.
- **No "we're excited to announce".** If the news is good, it is self-evident. Lead with what changed.

## Signature verbs & phrases

Three verbal patterns recur across lavder.com and should anchor future copy. Do not overuse, but reach for them when a section needs a verb:

- **Costruiamo** / *We build* — used for bespoke, long-form work (*"Costruiamo ogni progetto attorno alle tue esigenze"*).
- **Trasformiamo** / *We transform* — used when the subject is a state-change for the client (*"Trasformiamo idee in design visivi"*, *"Trasformiamo la tua presenza sui social"*).
- **Su misura** / *Bespoke* / *Custom-fit* — recurring qualifier. Anywhere we offer a product (sites, e-commerce, software, campaigns), we offer it *su misura*.

**Positioning phrase — reusable across channels**:
> *Più che la tua agenzia web, vogliamo essere il tuo **partner di fiducia**.*
> *More than an agency. Your trusted partner.*

**Tagline with emphasis** (visual treatment):
> Tutti lo fanno. ***Noi lo facciamo*** bene.

The middle clause ("Noi lo facciamo") is typeset in **italic** or in a visually contrasting weight/color. Plain-weight rendering is acceptable in constrained contexts (favicon-sized, plain-text email) but not preferred.

## Italian voice

Italian copy follows the same rules with two local adjustments:
- Prefer *voi* for B2B (formal-plural), *tu* for B2C consumer products (Dolce, for example).
- Avoid anglicisms when a plain Italian word works better. *Risultato* beats *output*. *Strumento* beats *tool*. But keep industry terms (*web*, *marketing*, *brand*) — translating them makes us sound dated.

## Romanian voice

Romanian copy uses formal *dumneavoastră* in B2B contexts. Mirrors the Italian rules: plain over ornate, short over long, concrete over abstract.

## Examples

### Headline

- ✅ *Build once. Scale forever.*
- ✅ *Siti web che funzionano davvero.*
- ❌ *We help businesses unlock their full potential through innovative digital solutions.*

### Empty state

- ✅ *No projects yet. Start your first one.*
- ❌ *Oh no! It looks like you haven't created any projects yet. 😢 Let's get started on your exciting journey!*

### Error

- ✅ *Couldn't save. Check your connection and try again.*
- ❌ *Oops! Something went wrong. Please try again later!*

### Legal footer

- ✅ *Lavder Enterprise Srl. Registered in Bucharest. VAT RO00000000.*
- ❌ *Copyright © Lavder Enterprise. All rights reserved worldwide.*
